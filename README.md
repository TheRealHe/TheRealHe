## About Me

I'm a developer who's genuinely optimistic about the future of artificial intelligence. I believe AI is here to stay, and that its real power lies in helping us automate processes, solve meaningful problems, and free people up to focus on what truly matters.

To me, AI isn't just a tool; it's a beautiful and fascinating field I want to dedicate most... if not all of my career to.

### My Path Forward

- ✅ **Build useful AI tools**  
  Already achieved with [Remi-Subtitle-Forge](https://github.com/TheRealHe/Remi-Subtitle-Forge) a multilingual subtitle generator powered by Whisper and NLLB-200.

- 🚀 **Deploy effectively to users around the world**  
  My next goal: take what I've learn and build something accessible, scalable, and truly useful for people across the globe.

- 🧠 **Master the full AI lifecycle**  
  The ultimate objective: gain professional-level expertise in building, deploying, training, and understanding AI models from the ground up.
